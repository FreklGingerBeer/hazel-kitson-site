The work

Four phases, plainly named.

Each phase is quoted as its own line item. Approval at the end of one phase is the trigger to start the next.

01 Category & Concept

What's actually moving on shelf.

Category scan across non-alc, wine, spirits, RTD, beer and functional. Gap analysis, positioning frame, three concept routes with rough cost windows.

02 Recipe & Makers

The thing, and who builds it.

Food technologists, ingredient suppliers and contract manufacturers matched to scale. Iteration on the chosen concept to a final, costed formulation.

03 Rules & Packaging

FSANZ, claims, certifications.

Nutrition panels, allergen statements, certifications you need (halal, kosher, organic). Packaging sourced and locked. Artwork to print.

04 Production & Launch

Honest unit economics.

First production scheduled. COGS, MOQs, landed cost reconciled against the formulation. Hand-off to the buyer conversation.
